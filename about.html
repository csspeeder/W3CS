About WECS

About

WECS Engineering is my personal engineering space. It is built around one simple idea: turning ideas into real physical prototypes. WECS is not a company pretending to be bigger than it is — at least not yet. It is simply a place to show my builds, my process and the projects I create.

Principle

Build first. Justify later.

WECS follows a simple engineering philosophy: if an idea seems unnecessary, overcomplicated or slightly unreasonable, it is probably worth building, whatever the bullshit may be. The only limits are imagination, physics, budget and the rare moment where common sense decides to show up. Morals are considered optional equipment.

Focus

Robotics, mechanics and embedded control.

The focus is not only making prototypes look finished. The focus is understanding trade-offs: stiffness, mass, power, control, fitment and iteration.

About WECS

About

WECS is a personal engineering portfolio for physical prototypes: robotics, mechanical design, embedded systems and 3D-printed hardware.

Principle

Build real systems. Test them honestly.

WECS documents the complete engineering path: sketch, CAD, electronics, code, mechanical weak points, failed versions and the next improved build.

Focus

Robotics, mechanics and embedded control.

The focus is not only making prototypes look finished. The focus is understanding trade-offs: stiffness, mass, power, control, fitment and iteration.
